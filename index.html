Hitch Widget

The CURT Hitch Widget will allow you to deploy a hitch lookup directly onto your site with just a couple lines of code that you can copy and paste. It will contain all live data (currently front and rear receiver hitches along with associated products) from the CURT Manufacturing database. This means that as soon as we add a product to our database, it shows up on your site. You can also customize the Hitch Widget to work with the shopping cart on your site or integrate it with Google Checkout or PayPal.
