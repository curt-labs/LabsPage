Hitch Widget

The CURT Hitch Widget will allow you to add a hitch lookup to your site by simply copying and pasting a couple lines of code onto a page in your website. It has built in integration with Google Checkout, PayPal, and possible your shopping cart.*

Benefits

  • Live data - All data that is fed into the Hitch Widget is live data being pulled directly from CURT's servers. The data currently includes front and rear mount receiver mount receiver hitches and associated products.
  • Easy integration - Simple copy and paste of a couple lines of code and your ready to go.
  • Shopping Cart - Integrates with Google Checkout, Paypal, or your shopping cart.*
  • Version 1.x Pricing - You have the ability to set a global price increment based on list price.
  • Pricing* - Set custom pricing on a per product basis.
  • * Available in Version 2.0 only.

Limitations

  • Customization - You do not have the ability to customize the look and feel of the Hitch Widget without CURT's eCommerce developers creating a custom version.
  • One Item purchasing - If a customer would like to purchase more than one product, they have to go through the vehicle lookup several times. The exception being Google Checkout.

Version 2.0.0

This version of the solves a couple of known issues with previous versions of the Hitch Widget. You can control pricing on an item specific level. You can also incorporate multi-item checkout with Google Checkout. This means that the user no longer needs to go through the lookup each time for every product that they wish to purchase. This version is the easiest to implement and is the recommended implementation.
